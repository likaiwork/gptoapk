## How to Create a Facebook Account

### Account Requirements

1. Open the Facebook app or visit **facebook.com/r.php**

   - Date of birth
   - Gender
3. Click **"Sign Up"**

5. Add a profile photo
6. Find friends (optional — skip this step if you want)

### Tips for Successful Registration

- ✅ Use a **real** name — fake names get flagged
- ✅ Use Gmail or Outlook for registration (most reliable)
- ❌ Don't use temporary email services
- ✅ Complete your profile within 24 hours
- ✅ Add a profile picture — accounts with photos are less likely to be flagged

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Is Facebook free to download?
Yes. Facebook is completely free to download and use. Meta makes money primarily from advertising.

### Q2: Does Facebook work in China?
Facebook.com is blocked in mainland China. You'll need a VPN or proxy to access it.

### Q3: Can I use Facebook without a phone number?
Yes. You can register with an email address instead. Gmail and Outlook work best.

### Q4: What's the difference between Facebook and Messenger?
Facebook is the main social platform. Messenger is a separate chat app. You need a Facebook account to use Messenger.

### Q5: How do I update Facebook on Android?
- Google Play: Open Play Store → My apps → Facebook → Update
- APK: Download the latest version and install over the existing one
- Settings: App info → Facebook → Check for updates

### Q6: Why does Facebook keep crashing?
Try these fixes in order:
1. Clear cache (Settings → Apps → Facebook → Clear cache)
2. Update to latest version
3. Restart your phone
4. Uninstall and reinstall

### Q8: Facebook keeps saying "Something went wrong"?
